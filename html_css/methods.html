Methods

Figure S1. Schematic of study. There were 10 patients in our study. Peripheral blood was drawn from each patient, post HIV infection, for 10-20 longitudinal time-points. Each blood sample was divided into PBMCs and plasma. Ab sequences were derived from the PBMCs and HIV sequences were derived from plasma. Note that patient avatars in no way reflect actual patients and instead reflect one lonely scientist. *Only patients 1, 2, and 5 had the final time-point with ART exposure.
